What is GoldCard?

GoldCard is a groundbreaking fintech solution that lets you own, spend, and manage real gold instantly. Your account balance is denominated in grams of physical gold, which you can spend anywhere using a virtual or physical debit card, just like cash. All gold is securely stored, 100% asset-backed, and fully Shariah-compliant.
